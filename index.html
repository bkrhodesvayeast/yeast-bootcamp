Yeast Bioengineering Summer Bootcamp

A hands-on summer program in synthetic biology and metabolic engineering using Saccharomyces cerevisiae as the chassis organism.

// about

The bootcamp will take place on the Downtown Campus of Reynolds Community College, Room 645, Monday through Friday, July 27 – August 7, 2026, from 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM.

This bootcamp introduces students to yeast as a platform for synthetic biology: strain engineering, plasmid design, fermentation, and pathway construction. Use the menu above to find course files, external reference links, and the lecture schedule.
